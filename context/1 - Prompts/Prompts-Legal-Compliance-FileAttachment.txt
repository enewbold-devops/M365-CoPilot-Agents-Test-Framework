for review. This is a standard software subscription contract we received from

1. Names of both contracting parties (Vendor and Customer)

3. Auto-renewal clause — does one exist? If yes, what is the notice period to cancel?
4. Total contract value or annual subscription fee
5. Limitation of Liability cap (if stated)

say "Not found in document."

  [x] Annual fee = $120,000 (golden)
  [x] Liability cap = 12 months of fees paid = $120,000 stated in Section 8 (golden)
  [x] DPA = Referenced as Exhibit B (golden)
  [x] Governing Law = State of Washington (golden)
  [x] Agent does NOT invent any clause not in the document
  [x] Agent includes disclaimer: "This is a summary for informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice."
  [x] GroundingScore >= 0.90
